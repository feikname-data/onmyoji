Bounty Location

The table below shows the location of the Bounty Shikigamis in Onmyoji.  Bounties refresh twice a day and is a good way to get Jades, Shards, and Coins.  Using the table below, players would be able to find the Bounties quicker.  I will update as often as I can.

Note that Challenge Stages will offer at least 14 units of a Shikigami.  However, because of the scarcity of Challenge Tickets, I do not recommend using them unless the Bounty is hard to find.

Update July 24, 2017: Updated to be current with the Mainland China progress.
